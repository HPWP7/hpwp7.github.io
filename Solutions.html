Insurance Central

InsuranceCentral is a total solution for General Insurance Companies, specifically designed for the needs of the Cyprus market. It provides extended functionality packaged into a number of closely integrated modules allowing it to scale up to accommodate big companies as well as down to accommodate smaller companies. Modules include General Insurance, Claims Management, Insurance Accounting, CustomerWeb, AgentWeb and Document management. With an extensive client base of many Insurance companies we continuously maintain, enhance and tailor our solution to meet our client's needs. Recent changes include compliance changes for support of regulations for premium collection by the Superintendent of Insurance as well as enhancements to payment options with direct debit and credit card payments for installments to automate the collection of premiums.

Online Payments

We have extensive expertise in payment solutions both as a consumer of APIs from jcc, paypal and others as well as having our own server APIs with full implementations of ISO8583 payments protocol for our private label Card system to custom api workflows for connections to banking and money transfer networks through our work on MoneyTransfer solutions, as well as SEPA Direct Debit integrations supported by our insurance and accounting systems.
